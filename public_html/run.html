Ken 'Ya Run 5K ⁄ 1K
Saturday, November 18, 2017 • 8:00AM Walk ⁄ 8:30AM Run
Still Family Farm

Run Information

Course Description

This is not your typical road race or trail run! The entire race will be off-pavement, and will wind through a combination of farmland and beautiful woods. The course consists of a nice combination of flats and hills.

T-shirts

All runners registered by November 11, 2017 are guaranteed a super soft race t-shirt on race day. Please register by November 11, 2017 to guarantee your race shirt! All participants registered after that date will receive a shirt on a first come basis.

Prizes

Prizes will be awarded for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place overall male and female as well as 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place male and female in the following age groups:
  • 10 and under
  • 11-19
  • 30-39
  • 40-49
  • 50-59
  • 60+

Parking

There is ample parking on site.

Timing

The race will be manually timed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When can I pick up my bib number?

A: You can pick up your packet early at Big Peach Running Co. in Kennesaw on Friday, November 17th, from 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM. The address is 1625 Ridenour Boulevard, Kennesaw, GA 30152. Or you can pick it up race day starting at 7:00 AM.

Q: Do I have to run in order to get a t-shirt?

A: No, phantom runners are welcome. Each Phantom runner will have there shirt mailed to them.

Q: Are dogs allowed?

A: Due to some narrower areas on the trail, we ask that you not bring pets.

Q: Is the course stroller friendly?

A: Unfortunately, no.

Q: Will there be a water station on the course?

A: Yes, and due to a loop in the course, you pass it at around the 3K mark.

Q: Is smoking allowed?

A: No. Still Family Farm is a no smoking venue.

Q: The race is on a farm. Do I have to have a 4x4 to park there?

A: No. There's plenty of easy parking that's accessible to all vehicles.
