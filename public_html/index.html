COME JOIN US FOR THE
2017 KEN 'YA RUN

Saturday, November 18th

Walk at 8:00 AM
Run at 8:30 AM

Welcome to the second annual Ken 'Ya Run event. Take a moment to enjoy the results of last year's event.



Come out on Saturday, November 18, to run (5K) or walk (1K) the trails on the beautiful Still Family Farm. Situated on 149 acres in Powder Springs, the farm trail provides a refreshing run through beautiful countryside.

Registration Fees

We are expecting a sellout crowd for this race so please register early to ensure your spot.

Regular Registration: $25/$20

Through Saturday November 11th you can register on-line for the cost of

  • 5K Run/Walk: $25
  • 1K Fun Run/Walk: $20

Late Registration: $30

From Sunday November 12th to Friday November 17th you can register on-line for the cost of $30 per runner/walker.

Same Day Registration: $30

On-site registration the day of the run will be $30 per runner/walker.

Phantom Runners: $30

Phantom runners are welcome as well, we even mail you the shirt!!
