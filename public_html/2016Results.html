2016 KEN 'YA RUN
The Results Are In


Saturday, November 19, 2016

A HUGE shout out to our Sponsors and Runners (Walkers). We did even better than we ever could have hoped!! And it was all due to your help and support.

After all the numbers, we have cleared nearly $5,500 for the Technical School. This equates to some 550,000 Kenya Shillings. To put this into perspective, $1.50 will get you half a chicken and a plate of fries for lunch. So this will go a LONG way towards windows and doors and wiring and other needs.

On behalf of KCK and the Race Team: Thank you all.
